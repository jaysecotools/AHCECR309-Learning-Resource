Revision & Quiz

Test your knowledge of ecological and cultural site inspection procedures.

Quick Quiz

Question 1: What is the first step in conducting an ecological site inspection?

A. Document all plant species
B. Assess soil conditions
C. Review existing information about the site
D. Look for wildlife
Correct! Reviewing existing information helps you know what to look for during the inspection.

Question 2: Which legislation primarily protects Aboriginal heritage in Tasmania?

A. Environmental Protection Act
B. Aboriginal Heritage Act
C. Nature Conservation Act
D. Land Use Planning Act
Correct! The Aboriginal Heritage Act 1975 is the primary legislation protecting Aboriginal heritage in Tasmania.
