Purpose of the Tool

The AHCECR309 Learning Resource is designed to help you gain the knowledge and skills needed to carry out an ecological and cultural site inspection. The tools and modules simulate real-world tasks so that you can practice safely before going into the field. By working through the activities, you will learn how to observe, record, and assess environmental and cultural features with confidence.

Learning Outcomes

By completing this learning resource, you will be able to:

  • Explain the purpose of ecological and cultural site inspections.
  • Identify key Tasmanian ecosystems and recognise their features on a map.
  • Recognise cultural heritage sites and understand their importance.
  • Apply relevant legislation and guidelines during inspections.
  • Identify threats such as weeds, erosion, and human impacts.
  • Accurately complete a site inspection report using forms, checkboxes, and dropdowns.
  • Review your own learning through quizzes, flashcards, and revision activities.
